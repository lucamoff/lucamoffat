CV 

Hello! I am a Research Intern at Bruegel. Before joining Bruegel, I worked as a Research Intern at the Economic Policy Research Centre in Kampala, Uganda. I finished my undergrad in Spring 2022, graduating from McGill University with first class honours in Economics and Russian Studies.

In Kampala, I worked in the Sectoral Department, studying the impact of Uganda's oil and gas exploration on the local agricultural sector. Since Spring 2021, I have also been working with Professors Charles Gottlieb and Markus Poschke on their large work-in-data project, creating and managing large datasets in order to study multiple issues, one example being barriers to structural change out of agriculture due to low female bargaining power.

Aside from that, I like taking photos :)
